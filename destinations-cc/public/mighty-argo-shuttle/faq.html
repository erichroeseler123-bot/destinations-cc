Common Questions

Can I do multiple gondola rides?

Yes, with a day pass from Mighty Argo, you can do unlimited laps. Our shuttles have flexible returns to accommodate.

Do I need to book bikes in advance?

Recommended for groups, but we have limited on-site rentals. Add at booking for $50/day.

What if traffic delays the shuttle?

We allow a 1-hour buffer for I-70. Real-time updates via text.

Is the Red Rocks stop optional?

It's included on all returns, but quick (30 min) for photos/views.

Group discounts?

Yes, $45/person for 4+. Contact us for custom packages.
